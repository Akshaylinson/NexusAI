Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about NEXUS AI Platform

How does NEXUS ensure data security and compliance?

NEXUS employs multiple layers of security including end-to-end encryption, zero-trust architecture, and quantum-resistant algorithms. We are compliant with GDPR, HIPAA, SOC 2, ISO 27001, and other major regulatory frameworks. All data is processed in certified secure environments with strict access controls and audit logging.

What is the implementation timeline for enterprise deployment?

Implementation typically takes 4-8 weeks depending on complexity and integration requirements. Our process includes discovery, configuration, integration, testing, and training phases. For Elite tier customers with on-premise deployment, the timeline may extend to 12-16 weeks to accommodate infrastructure setup.

Can NEXUS integrate with our existing legacy systems?

Yes, NEXUS offers over 500 pre-built connectors for common enterprise systems (ERP, CRM, HRIS, etc.) and provides flexible APIs for custom integrations. Our platform is designed with an API-first architecture that supports both modern microservices and legacy monolithic systems through adapters and middleware.

What level of customization is available for AI models?

NEXUS offers three levels of model customization: 1) Pre-trained models fine-tuned with your data, 2) Custom model architecture designed for specific use cases, and 3) Full proprietary model development for unique business problems. Our data science team works closely with Enterprise and Elite customers to develop tailored solutions.

What kind of support and training do you provide?

All plans include comprehensive documentation, video tutorials, and community forums. Professional tier includes email support with 24-hour response time. Enterprise tier adds priority phone and chat support with 4-hour response SLA. Elite tier provides 24/7 dedicated support with a named customer success manager and quarterly business reviews.
